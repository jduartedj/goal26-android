⚽ Goal26 — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Overview

Goal26 ("the App") is a free, ad-supported application for tracking the FIFA World Cup 2026. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information. The App does not require account creation, login, or any personal data input.

The following data may be collected automatically:

  • Analytics data: Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage data (screens viewed, session duration, device type) to help us improve the app.
  • Advertising data: Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Favorites: Your favorite teams are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for advertising (AdMob Privacy)
  • Firebase Analytics — for anonymous usage analytics (Firebase Privacy)
  • WC2026 API — for World Cup match data (no personal data shared)

Data Storage

App preferences (theme choice, favorite teams) are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. Match and team data is cached locally using a SQLite database for offline access.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can clear all locally stored data by clearing the app's data or uninstalling it. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your Android device settings under Settings → Google → Ads.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
